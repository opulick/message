Bee Facts

Honey bees and bumble bees have been around for about 30 million years. There are only 7 species of honey bee that is recognized and are are 44 total subspecies. Honey bees and bumble beer are social workers and they live together. Bees are esstential for keeping alive the ecosystem. However most bees are solitary. Both honey bees and bumble bees have one queen and workers. The honey beers have about thousands workers, mostly female workers with a small portion of male workers. The bumble bees have a much smaller nest as small as few hundred.

The honey bees navigate through the hair on their eyes. The hair on their eyes can sens the wind current and it helps them assit in navigation. Have you noticed that their eyes are hexagonal just like the bee hives. They are able to distribute pollen evenly on their hind legs.
